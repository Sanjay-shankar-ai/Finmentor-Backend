Q: How can I check my account balance?
A: You can use net banking, chatbot, or dial *99#.

Q: My debit card is not working — what should I do?
A: Check expiry, sufficient balance, and ATM network status.

Q: What is the interest rate on savings accounts?
A: Usually 3–4% annually (varies by bank).

Q: What are the charges for UPI transactions?
A: Currently nil for P2P; merchants may incur MDR.

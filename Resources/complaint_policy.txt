Complaint Handling Policy:
1. Customer lodges complaint via app, email, or chatbot.
2. Complaint is categorized: card issue, fraud, transaction delay, etc.
3. TAT (Turnaround Time):
   - Normal queries: 3 working days
   - Escalated issues: 7 working days
4. User receives ticket ID and status updates.
5. If unresolved, escalate to Grievance Officer or Banking Ombudsman.

GST Summary:
- GSTR-1: Monthly/quarterly outward supplies (sales).
- GSTR-3B: Summary return with tax payment.

Input Tax Credit (ITC):
- You can claim credit for GST paid on business purchases.
- Must match invoices uploaded by suppliers (GSTR-2B).

Due Dates:
- GSTR-1: 11th of next month.
- GSTR-3B: 20th of next month.

Penalties:
- Late fee ₹50/day (₹20 if nil return), interest 18% p.a.
